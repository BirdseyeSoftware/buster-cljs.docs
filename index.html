buster-cljs

An awesome platform for Clojurescript testing

Get Started! » Read doc guides » Contribute »

Clojure-like

buster-cljs offers an API similar to the one provided by clojure.test, so it won't be hard to get started.

Crossplatform

You can share your testsuite easily between Clojure and Clojurescript without to much of a hassle.

Extensive testing

buster.js is huge platform that helps you execute easily test suites on multiple browsers (desktop or mobile), and node.
